Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

This is the personal landing page for the Noah Bristol YouTube channel. This policy explains, in plain language, what happens with your information when you submit this form.

Signup Form

This site includes an embedded signup form powered by Kajabi. If you fill it out, whatever you submit (such as your name and email) is sent directly to and stored by Kajabi, not by this website. Kajabi's own privacy practices govern that data — see their privacy policy for details.

Your email is used to send you the resource you requested. If you also confirm the optional subscription in that email, we'll occasionally send you updates. You're never required to subscribe to get the resource. Kajabi is based in the United States, so submitting the form means your data is transferred there; Kajabi provides safeguards for this (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) as described in their privacy policy.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, data protection laws (such as the GDPR in the EU/UK) may give you specific legal rights over your personal data. Regardless of your location, we're happy to honor the following on request:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct it if it's inaccurate
  • Erase it (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to how it's processed
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time, for example by unsubscribing via the link in any update email
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address below and we'll action your request directly with Kajabi, where the data is stored.

Third-Party Resources Loaded by This Site

This page loads a couple of resources from outside servers in order to display correctly:

  • Google Fonts — the page font is loaded from Google's servers, which means Google can see that a request was made.
  • A favicon icon from svgrepo.com — the small icon shown in your browser tab is loaded from svgrepo.com.

Loading these means those services can see basic request information (like your IP address), the same as any request to any website. This site does not control what those third parties do with that information.

No Analytics or Tracking

This site does not run its own analytics or tracking scripts. We don't know who visits, how many people visit, or what you do on the page.

Hosting & Server Logs

If and when this site is hosted on a platform (such as GitHub Pages), that platform may keep standard web server logs (like IP address, browser type, and timestamps) as part of normal operation. That's handled by the hosting provider, not by us directly.

Children's Privacy

This site isn't directed at children and doesn't knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.
