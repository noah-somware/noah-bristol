# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 6, 2026

This is the personal landing page for the Noah Bristol YouTube channel. This policy explains, in plain language, what happens with your information when you visit.

## Signup Form

This site includes an embedded signup form powered by Kajabi. If you fill it out, whatever you submit (such as your name and email) is sent directly to and stored by Kajabi, not by this website. Kajabi's own privacy practices govern that data — see their [privacy policy](https://kajabi.com/privacy-policy) for details.

## Third-Party Resources Loaded by This Site

This page loads a couple of resources from outside servers in order to display correctly:

- **Google Fonts** — the page font is loaded from Google's servers, which means Google can see that a request was made.
- **A favicon icon from svgrepo.com** — the small icon shown in your browser tab is loaded from svgrepo.com.

Loading these means those services can see basic request information (like your IP address), the same as any request to any website. This site does not control what those third parties do with that information.

## No Analytics or Tracking

This site does not run its own analytics or tracking scripts. We don't know who visits, how many people visit, or what you do on the page.

## Hosting & Server Logs

If and when this site is hosted on a platform (such as GitHub Pages), that platform may keep standard web server logs (like IP address, browser type, and timestamps) as part of normal operation. That's handled by the hosting provider, not by us directly.

## Children's Privacy

This site isn't directed at children and doesn't knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

This policy may be updated from time to time as the site changes. Check back here for the current version.
